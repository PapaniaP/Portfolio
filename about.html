Paolo

Hi! I'm Paolo, a 28-year-old South African studying Multimedia Design in Aarhus. I'm on a mission to become a frontend developer, using my knowledge of UX/UI design and my love for technology.

While I'm still polishing up on my coding skills, I have many years of experience as a teacher. So I'm pro at working with people, communicating and working in a team.

When I'm not studying, coding, or designing you can find me playing video games, cheering for my rugby team, or building mechanical keyboards. I also have a zoo, which contains 2 dogs and 2 cats.

You can find me where pixels meet professionalism, innovation meets imagination and the art of frontend development converges with the science of UX design.
